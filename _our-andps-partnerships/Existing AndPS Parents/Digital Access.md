<p>DigitalAccess@Home provides subsidised broadband and laptops/tablets to
lower-income households.</p>
<p>More information on DigitalAccess@Home is available from IMDA website
[<a href="https://www.imda.gov.sg/how-we-can-help/digital-access-at-home](https://www.imda.gov.sg/how-we-can-help/digital-access-at-home)" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.imda.gov.sg/how-we-can-help/digital-access-at-home](https://www.imda.gov.sg/how-we-can-help/digital-access-at-home)</a>.</p>
<p>Parents can also find out more by:</p>
<p>1. Calling IMDA hotline at 6377 3800</p>
<p>2. Email [<a href="mailto:digitalaccess@imda.gov.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">digitalaccess@imda.gov.sg</a>](
<a href="mailto:digitalaccess@imda.gov.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">digitalaccess@imda.gov.sg</a>)</p>
<p>3. Visiting SG Digital Community hubs at selected Community Clubs (CC)
and public libraries listed on [<a href="https://go.gov.sg/sgdcommhub](https://go.gov.sg/sgdcommhub)" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/sgdcommhub](https://go.gov.sg/sgdcommhub)</a>
</p>
