<p>MOE values the well-being of our teachers, who play a vital role in supporting
your child’s learning. Most parents work closely and respectfully with
our staff, and this partnership is greatly appreciated. The updated Engagement
Charter reinforces the importance of respectful interactions between parents
and school staff. Where behaviour is inappropriate or unreasonable, the
school will take appropriate steps, which may include advisories to cease
such behaviour or limiting further engagement.</p>
<p>In cases where staff safety or well-being is compromised, the school will
seek assistance from the relevant authorities, including the Police. For
serious incidents, legal action may be taken under relevant legislation,
including the Protection from Harassment Act. We thank parents for their
continued partnership in maintaining a safe and respectful school environment.</p>
