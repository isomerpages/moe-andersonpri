<h4>Learning Support Programme (LSP)</h4>
<p>The LSP is an MOE intervention programme led by specially trained teachers
to support selected Primary 1 and 2 students. Conducted in small groups,
30-minute daily lessons strengthen foundational English language skills
in a supportive and encouraging environment.</p>
<h4>School-based Dyslexia Remediation Programme (SDR)</h4>
<p>The SDR programme provides targeted support for Primary 3 and 4 students
with dyslexia. Conducted three times a week, the lessons focus on strengthening
reading, spelling and comprehension, and equip students with strategies
to learn more effectively and confidently.</p>
<h4>Reading Remediation Programme (RRP)</h4>
<p>The RRP supports selected Primary 3 to 5 students who need additional
help with reading and understanding texts. Lessons teach practical strategies
to improve comprehension and build confidence so students can better access
learning across the curriculum.</p>
