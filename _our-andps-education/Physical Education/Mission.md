<h4>Mission</h4>
<p>To nurture physically competent and confident students who enjoy lifelong
active and healthy living, guided by our L.I.G.H.T. values and a strong
foundation in movement, safety, and well-being.</p>
<p>Where every child moves, grows, and thrives — nurturing confident individuals
with strong character and a lifelong love for active living.</p>
