<p>At Anderson Primary School, the Aesthetics Department is committed to
nurturing every child as a confident and expressive learner through rich
and meaningful experiences in Art and Music.</p>
<p>We design purposeful learning that engages students in creative exploration,
skill development, and reflective expression, enabling them to communicate
ideas, appreciate diverse artistic traditions, and grow socially and emotionally.</p>
<p>Guided by the MOE Art and Music syllabi and grounded in 21st Century Competencies,
our curriculum cultivates communication, collaboration, critical thinking,
and self-management through authentic artistic experiences.</p>
<p>We strive to develop well-rounded individuals who find joy in the arts,
connect with others meaningfully, and appreciate both local and global
cultures through the universal language of artistic expression.</p>
