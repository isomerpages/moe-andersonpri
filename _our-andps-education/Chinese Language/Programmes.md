<h3>Key Programmes</h3>
<h4>Di Zi Gui CCE Lessons (弟子规）</h4>
<p><em>Di Zi Gui</em> is taught to all Primary 1 and 2 students during Chinese
Language (CL) Character and Citizenship Education (CCE) lessons, as well
as through mass lectures conducted by the Zhonghua Cultural Society. Through <em>Di Zi Gui</em>,
students learn values such as filial piety, respect, responsibility, resilience,
integrity, care, harmony, and diligence.</p>

<h4>P3 - P6 Higher Chinese Language (HCL)（高级华文）</h4>
<p>HCL is offered to Primary 3 and 4 students who have a strong grounding,
aptitude, and interest in Chinese Language (CL). It is also offered in
Primary 5 and 6 as an additional subject. The HCL syllabus aims to develop
students’ linguistic skills to a higher level of proficiency, as well as
deepen their cultural knowledge. Students taking HCL in Primary 5 and 6
will sit for both CL and HCL examinations.</p>
<h4>P3 &amp; P4 Mother Tongue Support Programme（母语辅助计划）</h4>
<p>The Mother Tongue Support Programme is a customised programme designed
to support Primary 3 and 4 students in learning the Mother Tongue Language
(MTL). With smaller class sizes, students receive more individualised attention
and have more opportunities to practise what they have learnt.</p>
<h4>Mother Tongue Fortnight（母语双周）</h4>
<p>During the MTL Fortnight, a range of activities is provided to enable
students to learn and use the Mother Tongue Language (MTL) in engaging
and meaningful ways. The programme is designed to create an environment
that encourages students to use Chinese Language (CL) and appreciate its
associated culture.This enhances the learning experience and fosters greater
interest in learning CL.</p>

<h4>Primary 3 Language and Cultural Camp（语文与文化营）</h4>
<p>P3 Chinese Language and Cultural Camp is a half-day camp. It provides
an immersion environment with authentic learning experiences for students
to learn and use Chinese Language and to appreciate the Chinese culture
through hands-on activities.</p>

<h4>P4 Cultural Performance Exposure Scheme (CPES) (“文化随意门”计划)</h4>
<p>The Cultural Performance Exposure Scheme (CPES) (“文化随意门”计划) aims to expose
students to Chinese dramas and Chinese language-related performances.</p>

<h4>P1-P3 MTL SOAR Programme（“和书一起飞”阅读计划&nbsp;）</h4>
<p>Research shows that children who enjoy reading often demonstrate high
levels of self-driven motivation. The benefits of reading extend far beyond
literacy skills, positively impacting socio-emotional skills, academic
success and preparing children for global competence. MTL SOAR is a structured
reading programme that seeks to instil students’ love for reading in MTL.</p>
