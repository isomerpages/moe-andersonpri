<p></p>
<p><strong>Form Teacher Guidance Period (CCE FTGP)<br></strong>CCE FTGP creates
intentional space for teachers to connect with every student – through
structured activities, reflections and discussions that deepen Teacher-Student
Relationships (TSR) and nurture supportive peer relationships.</p>
<p></p>
<p><strong>&nbsp;</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>Social Studies</strong>
</p>
<p>Social Studies and CCE FTGP work together to nurture informed, empathetic,
and responsible citizens. Through inquiry and authentic learning, Social
Studies ignites students’ curiosity about real‑world issues and builds
the critical lens for students to understand Singapore and engage responsibly
with the wider world.</p>
