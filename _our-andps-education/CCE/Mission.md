<p>At Anderson Primary, character comes first. Our CCE curriculum nurtures
Andersonians across four domains to flourish, contribute meaningfully,
and lead fulfilling lives.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>1. Good Character</strong>
</p>
<p>Developing a sound moral compass — thinking critically, making responsible
choices, and showing care and empathy for others.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>2. Resilience &amp; Social-Emotional Well-Being</strong>
</p>
<p>Understanding themselves, building healthy relationships, staying resilient,
and cultivating gratitude through life's experiences.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>3. Future Readiness</strong>
</p>
<p>Developing purpose, adaptability, and a lifelong learning mindset to navigate
future pathways with confidence.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>4. Active Citizenship</strong>
</p>
<p>Building national identity, awareness of Singapore's realities, and the
will to contribute to community and country.</p>
