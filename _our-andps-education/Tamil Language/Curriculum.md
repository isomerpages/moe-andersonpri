<p>The Tamil Language curriculum is designed to develop confident communicators
with a strong appreciation of the Tamil language and culture. We design
purposeful learning experiences and enact effective pedagogies to engage
learners meaningfully. Lessons are differentiated to address students’
readiness, interests, and learning profiles, ensuring that all learners
are appropriately supported and challenged.</p>
<p>ICT and meaningful resources are leveraged to create interactive and authentic
learning experiences, making learning relevant and engaging. Beyond the
classroom, students participate in enriching programmes such as Mother
Tongue Fortnight, as well as activities and competitions organised by Tamil
department and external partners, providing opportunities for application
of learning in real-world contexts.</p>
<p>Through these varied learning experiences, students develop curiosity,
self-directedness, and independence. They are guided to deepen their understanding
and appreciation of the Tamil language and its rich cultural heritage,
while growing as confident and reflective learners.</p>
