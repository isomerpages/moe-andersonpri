<h3>Key Programme</h3>
<h4>1:1 Personal Learning Device (PLD) Programme (Primary 3 to Primary 6)</h4>
<p>Our 1:1 Personal Learning Device (PLD) Programme empowers every Primary
3 to Primary 6 student with access to a school-prescribed device, ensuring
equal opportunities for all learners to benefit from high-quality digital
tools and curriculum-aligned resources.</p>
<p>Used under the careful guidance of our teachers, PLDs enrich learning
across subjects through interactive lessons, designed to facilitate collaboration,
encourage creativity, and promote learning through meaningful engagement.</p>
<p>At the same time, we believe that the best learning happens both on screen
and beyond it. Our students continue to learn through hands-on activities,
outdoor experiences, discussions, and exploration — with technology thoughtfully
integrated to deepen understanding, not replace real-world experiences.
It is truly the best of both worlds.</p>
<h4>Code for Fun (Primary 4)</h4>
<p>The Code for Fun programme gives our Primary 4 Andersonians 10 hours of
hands-on learning in computational thinking, coding, and emerging technologies
such as Artificial Intelligence (AI). Using the interactive Sphero Bolt,
students will:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Explore computational thinking and how it applies to everyday life</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Learn basic programming concepts such as loops, variables, functions,
conditionals, and debugging</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Strengthen logical thinking and problem-solving skills</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Discover how AI and other emerging technologies shape the world around
them</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Through fun and engaging activities, students will build confidence as
creative thinkers and future-ready learners.</p>
