<h4>Curriculum</h4>
<p>Our Digital Competencies and AI Literacy curriculum equips Andersonians
with essential digital skills and responsible technology habits from Primary
1 to Primary 6. Students progressively learn to use devices confidently,
manage files, create and share digital work, and develop keyboarding skills.
They are also introduced to Artificial Intelligence (AI) through age-appropriate
learning experiences that build awareness of how AI supports learning,
the importance of verifying information, and the safe and responsible use
of digital tools.</p>
