<h4>Mission</h4>
<p>To empower Andersonians to become confident, responsible and future-ready
digital learners by weaving <strong>digital competencies</strong> and <strong>AI literacy</strong> into
everyday learning, enabling them to think critically, create meaningfully
and use technology wisely in school and beyond.</p>
