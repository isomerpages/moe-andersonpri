<h4>Mission</h4>
<p>Anderson Primary School's Aesthetics Department nurtures confident, expressive
learners through meaningful Art and Music experiences. Aligned with MOE
syllabi and 21st Century Competencies, our curriculum fosters creativity,
collaboration, and critical thinking. We develop well-rounded individuals
who find joy in the arts, connect meaningfully with others, and appreciate
local and global cultures.</p>
