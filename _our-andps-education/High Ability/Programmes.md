<h4>Specialised Talent Development in the Anderson Academy</h4>
<p>This enrichment programme is offered to selected students beyond regular
curriculum hours. It is anchored on the Community Problem Solving (CmPS)
framework and process, engaging students in collaborative, project-based
learning to identify, analyse, and address authentic community issues.
Through structured inquiry, ideation, and action, students develop innovative
problem-solving capabilities alongside clarity and confidence in communicating
their ideas and solutions.</p>
