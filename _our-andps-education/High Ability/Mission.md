<h4>Mission</h4>
<p>To nurture students of diverse talents, fostering critical thinking, thoughtful
leadership, and social responsibility through meaningful learning experiences.</p>
<h4>Our Approach</h4>
<p>We nurture the talents of all Andersonians through a talent development
framework with a continuum of progressive learning experiences, from broad
exposure to specialised development, in various domains.</p>
<p>At the foundation, all students are provided with a range of opportunities
to discover and build new interests, developing students’ innate sense
of curiosity and joy of learning. The next level offers advanced learning
opportunities that deepen skills and understanding for students ready to
be further challenged. At the peak, specialised talent development supports
students in pursuing their strengths at a higher level, enabling them to
soar with passion and purpose.</p>
