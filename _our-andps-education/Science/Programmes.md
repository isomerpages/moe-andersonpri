<p>Our curriculum is enriched with a variety of mandatory and elective programmes
designed to bring Science to life beyond the classroom.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Sci4.png">
</div>
<h3>Base Tier</h3>
<h4>Broad Exposure: Science for All</h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Integrated Suite of Resources</strong> for Textbook, Activity Book
and SLS resources. This serves as the foundational pedagogical ecosystem
for Science at Anderson Primary School. Its purpose is to provide a seamless,
multi-modal learning experience that caters to different learning styles
while ensuring alignment with the national curriculum.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Hands-on practical through the use of <strong>SPARKLE KITS</strong> to provide
students with the physical materials needed to build their own understanding
of scientific concepts through direct experimentation rather than just
reading from a textbook.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Our <strong>Young Scientist Badge Scheme</strong> encourages students to
complete self-directed tasks to earn badges. It stimulates a natural curiosity
about environmental and natural phenomena while nurturing positive dispositions
and attitudes toward scientific inquiry.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students embark on <strong>learning journeys</strong> through their primary
school journey. These journeys are intended to move a student from being
a passive recipient of information to an active participant in their environment,
ensuring that learning is not confined to the four walls of a classroom.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3>Middle Tier</h3>
<h4>Deepens Interest through Specialized Enrichment</h4>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>STEM Playgrounds </strong>to nurture curious and inquiring minds.
They are designed to bridge the gap between formal classroom learning and
informal, creative exploration.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Selected students go through <strong>E2K Science Programme</strong>, an
enrichment program that invites selected students to engage in advanced
scientific investigations.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3>Peak Tier</h3>
<h4>Interest-based Talent Development</h4>
<p>For students ready to be further challenged, we offer specialized talent
development pathways:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Science Competitions &amp; Fairs </strong>for selected students.
Students represent the school in prestigious events such as the Elementz
Science Project Competition, and various Science Olympiads.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Sustainability Projects</strong> by Eco-leaders. Students undertake
sustainability projects to improve the environment.</p>
</li>
</ul>
