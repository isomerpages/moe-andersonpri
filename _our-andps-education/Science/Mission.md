<h4>Mission</h4>
<p>Science education at Anderson Primary School nurtures <strong>future-ready learners</strong> with
a strong foundation in <strong>scientific literacy</strong>. We aim to develop <strong>curious </strong>and <strong>inquiring minds</strong> that
apply <strong>scientific inquiry</strong> to understand the <strong>natural and physical world</strong>,
while fostering a spirit of <strong>innovation</strong>, <strong>resilience</strong> and <strong>environmental stewardship</strong>.</p>
