<h4>Higher Mother Tongue Language (HMTL)</h4>
<p>A curriculum designed to stretch students who demonstrate strong aptitude
and interest in their Mother Tongue Language. It provides a more in-depth
curriculum beyond the standard MTL syllabus, incorporating complex texts,
ethnic literature, and higher-order thinking tasks to further extend and
challenge learners.</p>
<h4>Mother Tongue Support Programme (MTSP)</h4>
<p>A two-year initiative for Primary 3 and Primary 4 students, designed to
provide additional support for learners who require assistance in the learning
of their Mother Tongue Language (MTL).</p>
<h4>Mother Tongue Fortnight (MTF)</h4>
<p>An annual two-week programme with the aim of nurturing a love for the
Malay language among the students and to instill a sense of pride and connection
to their cultural roots. It provides meaningful learning experiences beyond
the classroom through hands-on activities and cultural performances, allowing
students to engage with language learning in an authentic and joyful.</p>
