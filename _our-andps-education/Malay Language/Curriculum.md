<h4>Malay Language Curriculum</h4>
<p>The Malay Language curriculum aims to develop confident communicators
with a deep understanding of Malay culture. Lessons are thoughtfully differentiated
to meet the needs of diverse learners, ensuring every student is supported
and challenged appropriately. We also leverage ICT to make classroom learning
more interactive and dynamic, bringing lessons to life. Beyond the classroom,
students participate in enriching programmes such as the Mother Tongue
Fortnight, as well as activities and competitions organised by external
organisations. These varied learning experiences inspire curiosity, foster
independence, and promote deeper, more meaningful learning of the language
and culture.</p>
