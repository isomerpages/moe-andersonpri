<p>Basketball CCA provides P3 to P6 students with opportunities to learn
fundamental skills and game techniques. Through training and matches, students
develop sportsmanship, teamwork and perseverance. Emphasising individual
progress and character growth, the programme builds confidence, discipline
and respect, nurturing responsible students who enjoy the game and strive
for continuous improvement.</p>
