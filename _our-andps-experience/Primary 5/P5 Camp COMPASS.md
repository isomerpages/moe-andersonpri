<p>The Primary 5 Camp COMPASS is a mandatory 3-day, 2-night in-school residential
programme where students apply physical and decision-making skills in an
authentic outdoor setting. They work collaboratively through communication
and mutual support with their peers and teachers, while developing tenacity,
confidence, and adaptability when facing challenges and uncertainty, strengthening
relationships and personal growth through the lived experience.</p>
