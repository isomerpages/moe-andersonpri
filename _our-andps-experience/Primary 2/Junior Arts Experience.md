<p>The Junior Arts Experience (JAE) at Anderson Primary School offers students
the opportunity to rotate through different Performing Arts experiences.
This exclusive programme exposes students to multiple Performing Arts disciplines,
broadening their skills, creativity, and appreciation for various art forms
while supporting holistic development and self-expression.</p>
