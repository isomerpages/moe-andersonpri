<p>The Junior Sports Experience allowed students to stay active and build
important skills through games like volleyball, badminton, soccer and basketball
in Anderson Primary. They learned the value of teamwork, coordination,
and perseverance while having fun with their peers.</p>
<p>The photos capture our students’ enthusiasm and joy as they participate
in these meaningful experiences, nurturing both their creativity and physical
development in a supportive environment.</p>
