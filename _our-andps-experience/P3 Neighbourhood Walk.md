<p>The Primary 3 Neighbourhood Walk prepares students for outdoor learning
by developing their ability to observe their surroundings safely and mindfully.
Through navigation activities in Yio Chu Kang Beacon, students practise
risk assessment and observational skills, while deepening their personal
connection with their school and immediate neighbourhood.</p>
