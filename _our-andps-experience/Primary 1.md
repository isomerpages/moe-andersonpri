<h4>P1 Anderson Experience: A Warm Welcome at the School of LIGHT </h4>
<p>Each year, we warmly welcome our Primary 1 students as they begin their
learning journey at Anderson Primary. With an inspiring welcome from our
Principal, Ms Shirley Leow, the support of parents, and the guidance of
dedicated teachers, our young learners explore the school, make new friends,
and enjoy their first recess together.</p>
<p>Anchored in our LIGHT values, this meaningful milestone sets the tone
for the year ahead — nurturing passionate learners, developing leaders
with heart, and shaping citizens of character.</p>
<p>Here’s to a joyful beginning and a purposeful year ahead — welcome to
the Anderson family!</p>
